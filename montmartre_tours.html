Highlight

Discover the hidden stories of Montmartre, a vibrant and artistic neighborhood See the famous Moulin Rouge, a world-renowned cabaret and entertainment venue Visit the house of French icon Dalida and see the picturesque Place Dalida Explore the charming streets of Montmartre and see the I Love You Wall Enjoy panoramic views of Paris from the Sacré-Cœur Basilica

Full description

Blanche Metro/Moulin Rouge: Start at the iconic cabaret. Amélie's Café & Van Gogh's House: Visit film and art history landmarks. I Love You Wall: Romantic stop. Bateau-Lavoir/Dalida: Explore artist history. Moulin de la Galette & La Maison Rose: Scenic windmills and cafes. Clos Montmartre & Lapin Agile: Vineyard and cabaret. Place du Tertre: Artist square. Sacré-Cœur Basilica: Panoramic views (guide ends here).

What to Expect

Footwear: Comfortable walking shoes are a must (expect hills!). Weather: Check the forecast and dress accordingly. Terrain: Be prepared for uphill walking. Photography: Capture the beauty, but respect private property. Security: Keep your belongings secure. Sacré-Cœur: The tour concludes here; explore the Basilica at your own pace.
