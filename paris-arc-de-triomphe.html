Grand Palais

The Grand Palais stands as a stunning glass-crowned masterpiece near the golden Pont Alexandre III. This architectural gem hosts world-class exhibitions and events beneath its magnificent glass dome.

Petit Palais

The Petit Palais, a breathtaking Beaux-Arts gem by the Seine, was built for the 1900 World’s Fair. Now home to the Museum of Fine Arts, it showcases masterpieces in an elegant, historic setting.

Arc De Triomphe

The Arc de Triomphe, built to honor Napoleon’s victories, stands as a powerful symbol of Parisian pride. Climb to the top for breathtaking panoramic views of the Champs-Élysées and beyond.
