Sainte-Chapelle, Joyau gothique

Discover Sainte-Chapelle, a breathtaking Gothic chapel in Paris, famous for its stunning stained-glass windows that depict over 1,100 biblical scenes. Located on Île de la Cité, this 13th-century royal chapel was built to house religious relics, including Christ’s Crown of Thorns. Visitors are mesmerized by its vibrant, sky-high glass panels, which create a magical light show. A top-rated attraction near Notre Dame, Sainte-Chapelle is a must-see for history lovers and architecture enthusiasts. Get skip-the-line tickets to explore this Parisian gem effortlessly!.
